Our small site welcomes friend links. Before applying for a friend link, please add our site to your friend links. I will add your link after the review is approved.

Application Guidelines

Requirements

  • Must have an independent domain name (the domain suffix must be on the public suffix list).
  • The entire site must support HTTPS.
  • The site must be created for more than six months (calculated from the publication date of the first blog post).
  • Must have more than 10 original articles.
  • The site must not belong to a “content farm” (see this article by Mr. Ruan for details).
  • Should not have excessive advertisements.

I look forward to exchanging friend links with sites that:

  • Can teach others new knowledge and skills.
  • Can share personal experiences, broadening others’ horizons and increasing their knowledge.
  • Can be updated regularly, allowing hobbies to continue over the long term.
